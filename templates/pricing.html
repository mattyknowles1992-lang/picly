How does the token system work? +
Tokens are credits you use for premium features. 1 token = ¬£0.008. Different features cost different amounts:
  • SDXL image: 2 tokens (¬£0.016)
  • DALL-E HD: 12 tokens (¬£0.096)
  • Cartoon video (8s): 35 tokens (¬£0.28)
  • Premium video (8s): 50 tokens (¬£0.40)
You can mix and match however you want! Flux and basic SDXL images are FREE and unlimited on paid plans.
What's the difference between cartoon and premium videos? +
Cartoon videos (8-9/10 quality): Perfect for animations, explainer videos, social media content, and stylized videos. Costs 30% less tokens, so you get 42% more videos!

Premium videos (10/10 quality): Photorealistic, cinema-grade quality for commercials, product demos, and professional content.

You can use both in the same plan! Mix cartoon videos for social media and premium videos for important projects.
Is it really unlimited images? +
Yes! Flux Schnell (8.5/10 quality) and SDXL + Refiner (9/10 quality) images are truly unlimited on all paid plans. Generate as many as you want - no caps, no throttling. Only premium features like DALL-E HD and videos use tokens.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely! No contracts, no commitments. Cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
What happens to unused tokens? +
Monthly subscription tokens reset each month. However, any tokens you purchase separately (token packages) never expire! Buy tokens once, use them whenever you want.
Do you offer refunds? +
Yes! We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on your first subscription. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 7 days for a full refund, no questions asked.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +
Yes! You can change plans anytime. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, the change takes effect at your next billing cycle.
Is there an annual plan? +
Coming soon! Annual plans will save you 20% compared to monthly billing. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when they launch.
