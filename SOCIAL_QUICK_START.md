## üéØ Best Practices

### Content Strategy
1. **Post Consistently** - 1-2 times/day minimum
2. **Mix Content Types** - 70% images, 30% videos
3. **Use Trending Topics** - Jump on viral trends fast
4. **Tell Stories** - Emotional connection = engagement
5. **Include CTAs** - "Save this post", "Tag a friend"

### Optimal Posting Times
- **Instagram:** 11 AM, 1 PM, 7 PM
- **Facebook:** 1 PM, 3 PM
- **Twitter:** 12 PM, 3 PM, 5 PM
- **LinkedIn:** 8 AM, 12 PM, 5 PM (weekdays)
- **TikTok:** 6 AM, 10 AM, 7 PM, 10 PM
- **Pinterest:** 2 PM, 9 PM

### Hashtag Strategy
- **Instagram:** 20-30 tags (use all available)
- **Twitter:** 1-2 tags only
- **LinkedIn:** 3-5 professional tags
- **TikTok:** 3-5 trending tags
- **Facebook:** 1-3 minimal
- **Pinterest:** 5-20 descriptive
